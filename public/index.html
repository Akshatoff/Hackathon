About Us

Filter is a cutting-edge app designed to verify the authenticity of information in the digital age. Users can input text prompts, and the app will meticulously analyze the content to determine its credibility. By leveraging advanced algorithms and real-time data sources, Filter can identify misinformation, fake news, and propaganda. Additionally, Filter's image analysis feature can detect if an image has been manipulated or generated using artificial intelligence, providing users with a powerful tool to combat the spread of visual disinformation.
